Frequently Asked Questions

1. How many types are available in the solar system?
There are three types of solar system. The first is on-grid, the second is off-grid and the third is a hybrid solar system.
2. Can a commercial solar system be used for home?
Yes, a commercial solar system can be used for home. A commercial system will produce more power and need more space to install. If you have that much space at your home and you can handle that much production then you can install a commercial solar system at your home.
